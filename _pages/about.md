I am a volcanologist and geochronologist interested in magma plumbing systems, eruptive histories, and volcano–ice interactions.

I recently completed my PhD at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. My research integrates ⁴⁰Ar/³⁹Ar geochronology, geochemistry, thermobarometry, and field volcanology to investigate how volcanic systems evolve through time.
